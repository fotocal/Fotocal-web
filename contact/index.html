Getting a good answer faster

What to put in the email.

None of this is required — send a one-line email if that is what you have and we will still reply. But if something is broken, these are the details that usually decide whether we can fix it in one reply or have to spend three emails asking you questions.

  1. Your phone and Android version. Settings → About phone. A surprising number of bugs only happen on one manufacturer's build.
  2. What you were doing. The screen you were on and the last thing you tapped before it went wrong.
  3. A screenshot. Worth roughly a page of description, especially for anything visual or for a scan that came back odd.
  4. The email on your account, if it is a subscription or account question — otherwise we cannot find you.

For a scan that got something wrong, the original photo helps a lot. It is the only way we can tell whether the model misread the food or the portion.
